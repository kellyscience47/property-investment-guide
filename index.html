📋 Deductible Expenses for Landlords

Standard Deduction: 43% of rental income (no receipts needed)

Itemized Deduction: Mortgage interest, management fee, insurance, repairs, depreciation, land value tax

Pro Tip: If actual expenses > 43% of rental income → use itemized. Otherwise, take the standard deduction.

🔄 Repurchase Tax Refund (重購退稅)

Sell your home and buy another within 2 years? Get your CGT refunded!

  • ✅ Both properties must be self-use
  • ✅ Buy within 2 years of selling
  • ✅ New price ≥ sale price → Full refund
  • ✅ New price < sale price → Proportional refund
